SECTION 1: VERBAL ABILITY

PART 5: PARAGRAPH ORGANIZATION

PRACTICE TEST 1 | 5-ITEM TEST | PASSING SCORE: 4

Direction: Determine which may be the best order in which the given sentences should be arranged to result in a well-organized paragraph.

1. Sentences:
I. Many Filipinos rely on public transportation for daily commuting.
II. Efforts to modernize public transport aim to make commuting more efficient.
III. Overcrowding and delays remain major concerns in urban areas.
IV. Modernization programs include new buses, e-jeepneys, and digital fare systems.

2. Sentences:
I. Virtual interviews became the norm for hiring during the pandemic.
II. Many companies discovered that remote recruitment saved time and resources.
III. As a result, some have decided to adopt virtual hiring permanently.
IV. However, not all industries find this method equally effective.

3. Sentences:
I. Proper waste segregation helps reduce landfill use and environmental damage.
II. Unfortunately, many households are unaware of how to properly separate waste.
III. Local governments are launching awareness campaigns to address this issue.
IV. This includes distributing guides and organizing seminars in barangays.

4. Sentences:
I. Deforestation contributes to climate change and biodiversity loss.
II. Reforestation efforts are being implemented across various regions.
III. These efforts aim to restore damaged ecosystems and absorb carbon dioxide.
IV. Environmental groups also involve communities in tree-planting activities.

5. Sentences:
I. The use of plastic bags has been banned in several cities.
II. Consumers are encouraged to bring reusable bags instead.
III. This is part of a larger movement to reduce plastic waste.
IV. Some businesses have responded by offering eco-friendly packaging.

Answer Explanations:

  1. A. I-III-IV-II
    Starts with commuting reliance → highlights the problems → shows modernization → explains the goal.
  2. C. I-II-IV-III
    Introduces virtual hiring → benefits → counterpoint → decision by some companies.
  3. A. I-II-III-IV
    Describes importance → highlights issue → explains solution → adds details.
  4. B. II-I-IV-III
    Begins with current efforts → gives reason (deforestation) → adds who helps → ends with the goal.
  5. B. III-I-II-IV
    Global movement → local response → consumer behavior → business adaptation.
